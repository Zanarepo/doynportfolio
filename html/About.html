About Me

I’m Adedoyin Adediran, an aspiring product manager with a passion for creating impactful solutions that bridge the gap between user needs and business goals. My journey into product management stems from my strong operational background across various industries, where I’ve honed my skills in project delivery, team collaboration, and stakeholder engagement. I’ve worked on exciting projects like AfriBite, a platform connecting African food lovers in the UK with authentic African restaurants, and ConnectPro, a hiring platform tailored for blue-collar professionals in Nigeria. These experiences allowed me to explore user research, design intuitive user flows, and build features that solve real-world problems. Another highlight is my work on RenegyHub, a renewable energy marketplace designed to connect sellers and buyers of eco-friendly products. From ideation to developing a seamless MVP, I collaborated with teams to deliver value-driven solutions. In these projects, I’ve utilized tools like Linear for tracking progress, Jira and Trello for managing workflows, and SurveyMonkey for user research. My approach is always user-centric, whether it’s designing onboarding processes, integrating payment gateways, or writing detailed product requirement documents (PRDs). I pride myself on being delivery-focused and data-driven, ensuring that every decision I make is grounded in insights and aligned with the overall product vision. My certifications in Product Management and Digital Marketing have equipped me with the skills to handle the end-to-end lifecycle of a product, from discovery to launch. Beyond projects, I’m a big believer in continuous learning and collaboration. I’m actively building my portfolio and sharing my journey to product management on LinkedIn, where I connect with like-minded professionals and share insights. My ultimate goal is to solve real problems with meaningful products that not only meet expectations but exceed them. If you’re looking for someone who is passionate, curious, and ready to make an impact, I’d love to connect and explore opportunities to create something extraordinary together.
